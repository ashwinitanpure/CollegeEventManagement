Quick Strike

The main objective of this event is to encourage the use of keyboard shortcuts for the tasks those are performed mainly using mouse.

The participants would be given some tasks that has to be done only through keyboard. No mouse would be provided.
There would be three rounds.
With each increasing rounds the level of difficulty increasing and there would be in all 3 rounds.

Rules:
  • The environment will be windows operating system.
  • The standard Qwerty keyboard will be provided.
  • Participants need to bring their College ID cards and the receipt of registration during reporting.
  • Time slots will be given and participants are expected to follow it strictly.
  • Participants are expected to give their correct contact details, so as to inform them about the results.
  • Further details of the round will be disclosed at the time of event.
  • Rules may be changed without prior intimation. Participants are requested to check the MindSpark'18 website (www.mind-spark.org) regularly for updates.
  • In case of any disputes, the decision of the organizers and the judges will be final and binding to all.
  • Team members may be from different colleges/institutions but one participant may not be a part of multiple teams for the same event.
